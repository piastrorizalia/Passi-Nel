The Story In The Book

Chapter 1

Molly had been gazing through her window for over an hour, her thoughts drifting like clouds beyond the glass. On her desk, surrounded by journals of science such as Nature, New Scientist, and many others, rested a worn copy of On The Road. It had been her loyal companion since her college years — a quiet reminder of freedom in the stillness of her confined world.

For ten long years, this room had been both her workshop and her prison. Above her hung a faded poster quoting Oscar Wilde: Work is the refuge of people who have nothing better to do. To others, Molly’s groundbreaking research decoding the mysteries of llama DNA was nothing short of brilliance. Yet in the quiet corners of her heart, she wondered if perhaps, she truly did have something better to do.
