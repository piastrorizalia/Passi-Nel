Pakbet

A classic Filipino vegetable dish simmered in bagoongâ€”rich, savory, and bursting with earthy flavors.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup squash (cut into cubes)
  • 1 cup eggplant (sliced)
  • 1 cup bitter melon / ampalaya (sliced)
  • 1 cup string beans (cut into 2-inch pieces)
  • 1 cup okra
  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 3 tbsp bagoong alamang
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Procedure

  1. SautÃ© garlic, onion, and tomato in a pot until fragrant.
  2. Add the squash first and cook for 3 minutes.
  3. Add string beans, ampalaya, okra, and eggplant.
  4. Pour in bagoong alamang and water.
  5. Cover and simmer until vegetables are tender.
  6. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
